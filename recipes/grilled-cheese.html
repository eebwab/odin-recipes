Butter Chicken

Back to all Recipes

Description

Grilled cheese is a sandwich that is filled with shredded or sliced cheese, then grilled on a hot surface. This makes the cheese nice and gooey, ready to be devoured along with a drink or soup of choice

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 slices of any bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese

Directions

  1. Melt butter and toast the bread
  2. Put cheese on one slice of bread, then toast place pieces of bread on top of each other
  3. Wait until cheese is melted
  4. Done, add hot sauce and tomato soup to garnish if desired
